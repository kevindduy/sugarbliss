The Heart of the Brand

Our Local Bakery Story

We are Danielle and Kylyn, a mom-and-daughter duo based in Zephyrhills. Sugar Bliss is a sanctuary of sweetness born from resilience.

Because Kylyn is immunocompromised, our standards require meticulous hygiene and strict safety. Every treat is crafted with the highest level of care, giving you a beautiful, safe, edible work of art.

Quality Guarantee

Strictly Pick-Up Only

The Florida heat is the enemy of delicate chocolate and sugar coatings. To guarantee a flawless, unmelted product, we strictly do not offer delivery.

⚠️ Customers must travel to us. We proudly serve residents of Wesley Chapel, Zephyrhills, and Dade City from our designated pick-up location.
